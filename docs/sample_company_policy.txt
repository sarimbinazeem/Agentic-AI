Northstar Tech — Remote Work Policy

Effective date: January 2025. All full-time employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.

Equipment

The company provides a laptop and monitor for home office setup. Employees must use company-approved VPN when accessing internal systems.

Core Hours

Employees must be available between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM in their local timezone.

Expense Reimbursement

Internet stipend of $50 per month is available for eligible remote employees.
